GOP Debate Visualization


This page visualizes the Twitter response to the August 6, 2015, Fox News Republican Debate.

Select a candidate to start exploring the data.


We've only included data from the top 10 polling candidates at the time, who participated in the 9pm debate (an additional debate starting at 5pm on the same date involved lower polling candidates)


We've calculated the number of positive and negative Tweets concerning each candidate and charted their overall sentiment score (the number of positive Tweets - number of negative Tweets) for each minute of the debate (note that we've excluded neutral tweets). At the bottom of the page, you can view candidates clustered by the topics with which they were frequently mentioned on Twitter to analyze which candidates Twitter users view as most similar and dissimilar.


The data used in this visualization comes from CrowdFlower Data for Everyone
